Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Please read the following information carefully before proceeding.

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

What happens when you delete your account:

  • All your personal data will be permanently deleted
  • Your service history and reviews will be removed
  • Any pending requests will be cancelled
  • You will lose access to any credits or rewards
  • You will need to create a new account to use Awhar again

How to delete your account:

  1. 1Open the Awhar app on your device
  2. 2Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account
  3. 3Confirm your decision by entering your password
  4. 4Your account will be scheduled for deletion within 30 days

Need help instead?

If you're experiencing issues with the app, our support team is here to help. Contact us before making a final decision.
